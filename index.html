This application is a mobile-based zakat and inheritance calculator that uses a rule-based expert system. It is designed to help Muslims easily calculate zakat obligations and inheritance distribution according to Islamic law. By entering user data, the app provides accurate calculations based on rules validated by zakat and inheritance experts.

The expert system ensures that the calculation results always comply with Islamic law, allowing users to easily understand and fulfill their obligations. This app not only helps with zakat calculations but also manages complex inheritance distributions, making it an effective tool for everyday use.
